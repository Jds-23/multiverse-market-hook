LMSR Calculator

Interactive explorer for Logarithmic Market Scoring Rule pricing mechanics.

1. Cost Function

The LMSR cost function aggregates outstanding token quantities into a single collateral value.

$$C(q_y, q_n) = b \cdot \ln\!\bigl(e^{q_y/b} + e^{q_n/b}\bigr), \quad b = \frac{\text{funding}}{\ln 2}$$
When $q_y = q_n$, the cost simplifies to $C = q + \text{funding}$.

2. Marginal Price (Sigmoid)

The marginal price of YES is the partial derivative of the cost function — a logistic sigmoid in the difference $q_y - q_n$.

$$p_{\text{yes}} = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(q_y - q_n)/b}}$$
Steeper curve = lower funding = more price sensitivity.

3. Net Cost of Trade

The net cost to buy $\Delta$ YES tokens is the difference in cost function before and after the trade.

$$\text{netCost}(\Delta) = C(q_y + \Delta,\; q_n) - C(q_y,\; q_n)$$

4. Token Cost Explorer

Overlay of marginal price and net cost curves. The shaded area under the marginal price curve from $0$ to $\Delta$ equals the net cost.

$$M(\delta) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(q_y + \delta - q_n)/b}}, \qquad N(\Delta) = \int_0^{\Delta} M(\delta)\,d\delta$$
$$\text{avg price} = \frac{N(\Delta)}{\Delta}, \qquad \text{slippage} = \text{avg price} - M(0)$$

5. Interactive Trade Calculator

Compute exact LMSR trade metrics. All values update live.

$$b = \frac{\text{funding}}{\ln 2}, \quad p_{\text{yes}} = \frac{e^{q_y/b}}{e^{q_y/b}+e^{q_n/b}}, \quad \text{netCost} = b\ln\!\frac{e^{(q_y+\Delta)/b}+e^{q_n/b}}{e^{q_y/b}+e^{q_n/b}}$$
